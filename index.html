Setup Calendar

Paste your Google Calendar public iCal URL to show today's events. You can find this in Google Calendar → Settings → your calendar → "Secret address in iCal format".

How to get this:
Google Calendar → ⚙️ Settings → Your calendar name → Scroll to Integrate calendar → Copy Secret address in iCal format
